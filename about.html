Who We Are

Company Overview

We are part of Josoon Electronics Co., Ltd, a supply chain and component sourcing company with a strong foundation in Asia and extensive experience supporting international customers.

Our Background

Founded in 2011, Josoon Electronics has developed long-term relationships across the electronic component supply chain, covering connectors, passive components, and active components.

With an experienced team, Josoon focuses on providing practical sourcing solutions for OEM and ODM customers, helping simplify complex procurement processes and improve supply efficiency.

What We Support

We support customers through integrated component sourcing, alternative part recommendations, flexible supply coordination, and long-term inventory support for critical components.

Our approach is centered on helping customers manage sourcing complexity while maintaining cost efficiency, stable lead times, and reliable product quality.

Global Customer Support Josoon Electronics has extensive experience supporting customers in Taiwan, Singapore, and Japan, while continuing to expand its service capabilities for North American customers.
Our Supply Network Through an established supply network across Asia, we are able to recommend competitive sourcing options from qualified manufacturers for stable and flexible supply support.
Our Role As part of Josoon Electronics, we focus on supporting North American customers through localized communication and coordination, bridging supply chain resources between Asia and overseas markets.

Who We Work With

Josoon Electronics has supported a wide range of customers across industrial, electronics, and technology sectors.
